Derrick Rose Era

Derrick Rose created a lot of buzz around Bulls basketball. Him and the hiring of coach Tom Thibodeau would lead the Bulls to 50-16 record and a first overall seed in the East. But Rose would tear his ACL in the first game of the series and was really never the same after that. Rose would be traded in 2016 and the Bulls have been rebuilding ever since.
